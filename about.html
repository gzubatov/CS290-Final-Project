About Us

Wrigley a.k.a "Mr. Wrigley Pigley"

Wrigley was born May 27th, 2015 and I picked him up in July 2015 in Indianapolis, IN. I drove all the way from Cleveland, OH to pick him up.

I named him Wrigley after my favorite baseball team, the Chicago Cubs, who play in Wrigley Field.

His nickname is "Wrigley Pigley" since he snorts a lot which can make him sound like a pig.

When I was working in Ohio I lived with roommates who had 3 large dogs and since Wrigley spent the first year of his life around these large dogs he seems to think he is also a large dog, even though he's only about 25lbs. However as a result, Wrigley only likes to play on the "Big Dog" side of the park when I take him on trips to our local dog park.

Boston Terriers are a very friendly breed, they are good with other dogs and small children. They make a great family pet. They love to lounge around and sleep all day, but they can also be pretty active. Wrigley is prone to getting the "zoomies" every once in a while and races around my apartment like a mad man.
